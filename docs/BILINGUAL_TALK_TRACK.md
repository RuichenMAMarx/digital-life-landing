# Bilingual Talk Track (ZH/EN)

## 1. Product Positioning
- 中文：我们不是做一个聊天玩具，而是做一个可运营、可衡量、可扩展的数字陪伴系统。
- EN: We are not building a chat toy. We are building an operable, measurable, and scalable digital companion system.

## 2. Why UID
- 中文：UID 是全链路主键，连接获客、绑定、素材采集、会话分配和留存分析。
- EN: UID is the primary key of the full funnel, connecting acquisition, binding, asset intake, channel allocation, and retention analytics.

## 3. Architecture Summary
- 中文：前端负责转化，Bot 负责交互，Control Plane 负责状态和分配。
- EN: Frontend handles conversion, bot handles interaction, and control-plane handles state and allocation.

## 4. Operations Summary
- 中文：运营核心是把“首次体验”做短，把“长期互动”做稳。
- EN: The operating core is to make first-time onboarding short and long-term interaction stable.

## 5. Monetization Summary
- 中文：短期用试用转订阅，中期做分层套餐，长期做 B2B 白标。
- EN: Short term: trial-to-subscription. Mid term: tiered bundles. Long term: B2B white-label.

## 6. Cost Summary
- 中文：成本主要来自模型调用、通讯通道费和人工运营。
- EN: Main costs come from model calls, messaging channel fees, and human operations.

## 7. KPI Summary
- 中文：我们看 6 个核心指标：跳转率、绑定率、素材完成率、留存、转化、回本周期。
- EN: We track 6 core KPIs: click-through, bind rate, asset completion, retention, conversion, and CAC payback.

## 8. Risk Statement
- 中文：我们承认生成质量和稳定性风险，但已经用库存和回退机制把体验下限兜住。
- EN: We acknowledge generation quality and reliability risks, but we already protect the UX baseline with inventory and fallback mechanisms.

## 9. Ask to PM
- 中文：希望 PM 帮我们优先定义“可证明商业价值”的实验路线和指标门槛。
- EN: We want the PM to help prioritize an experiment roadmap and metric gates that prove business value.
